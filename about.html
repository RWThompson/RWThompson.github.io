About Me

I am a recent BSc graduate with substantial experience of working with computers including fault diagnosis, database management, software development and computer building. I have always loved technology, however it was during my work experience that I realised that I wanted to have a career in IT as I found it so intriguing learning how computers worked and the various tasks that they can be used to perform; this made me want to take a computing course in college, where I was taught to code and is when I became truly passionate about the world of computing.

I chose to pursue a degree in computing as during my college computing course my passion for computers continued to grow; due to my thirst for learning more about the way in which computers work as well as learning how to develop software, and I wanted to develop the knowledge I had gained. My ambition within IT is to continue to progress my knowledge of computers and software development so that I can develop software, which people would use on a daily basis, to a very high standard.
