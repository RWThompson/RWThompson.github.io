Admin Interface

Commands:

"create [make] [reg] [year] [numberOfFaults]" - Creates a new car with inputted values

"check in [reg]" - Checks the car with the corresponding registration number into the garage

"output garage" - Outputs the cars in the garage

"calculate [reg]" - Calculates the bill for the car with a given registration number

"check out [reg]" - Checks the car with the corresponding registration number out of the garage

DO NOT PUT A SPACE BETWEEN THE TWO PARTS OF THE REG NUMBER!
